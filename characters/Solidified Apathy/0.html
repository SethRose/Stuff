Powers/Abilities


Vacuum Manipulation

To create and manipulate Vacuums of either spherical/egg shape, or the shape of the given area they are created in or around. Vacuums contain nothing, including air, and unless maintained carefully, air and other lighter objects will rush to fill the empty space with intense pressure and force, pulling anything and everything that can be swept away by heavy winds in with it. Collette most often uses this to create air currents.

Steel Air Generation

While the name is a tad misleading because it doesn't generate from nothing, this is the ability to sacrifice air in exchange for a clear, crystalline substance in any desired form that is as light as air and as hard and durable as steel. When not acted upon by blowing wind or hands, the steel rests in motion with the air. Air does not glide through it, it does provide wind resistance, and this causes it to move as fast as the wind current it's in and hit about as hard.
