Name:
S454
'Sara'

Affiliation:
Fenrir
Armored Suit Unit

Information:
An Armored Suit Unit within the ranks of Fenrir that is deployed during intense situations. Usually sent in a three-machine team, S454 is designated to work alongside any other manufactured ASU's and provide assistance with them in the field in combating or repelling A+ rank monsters that may come to attack the dome. They are described as 'quick and fast assassins', otherwise called the 'Ninja Wolves' of the Fenrir ASU foundation.
