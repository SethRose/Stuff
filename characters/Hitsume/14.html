Armored Suit Unit

Information:
An Armored Suit Unit, which is mostly comprised of machines and robots that are able to be combat operative and intense whenever commanded or deemed necessary for deployment. These are sent out in small teams of three in order to combat and resist rank S monsters temporarily to provide more time to Fenrir soldiers to retreat or do other things. They're expendable units, just costly and must be deployed at a reasonable time. This model number is designated to be 'C145', in which most people refer to them as 'Chief' as a shorter name. Thus far, there are only ten operable Armored Suit Units, C145 included.
