Name:
Caesar Kunioji

Affiliation:
Hope
Director

Information:
Former Onward soldier, Caesar transferred to being Hope personnel after a series of events that involved his former squad and their lives. Having originally aided the hospital as a normal medic, Caesar takes the role as Director because of the previous Director's sudden passing and was unable to deny the position in respects to the Director. He is depicted as a kind and humble man that tries to do for the best of many patients if it's within the limits of what he could do as a Director.
