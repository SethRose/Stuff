Powers/Abilities:
Aura User - Akahoshi utilises the power of different types of auras via accessing his chakras. The auras he's capable of utilising are:

Red Aura - Akahoshi possesses the Red Aura capability, otherwise known to him as Enhancement Aura. With this aura, he's capable of increasing their physical capabilities, but Akahoshi can take it a step further and increase certain properties of an object temporarily. He can do these things simultaneously. So far, in terms of increasing his own physical capabilities, he can go up to Peak Human (for a grown man), as for his senses, they can go to superhuman levels.

Yellow Aura - Akahoshi possesses the Yellow Aura capability, otherwise known as the Construction Aura to him. With it, he's capable of creating solid constructs with said aura and can control said constructs. It can also be utilised for Ergokinetic Combat or to create an Energetic Pressure. A bonus of this aura is that the user possesses a strong force of will. So far, in terms of his construction capabilities, he's only capable of creating up to human sized objects and if it's a weapon, it has to follow the Gun Regulation List.
