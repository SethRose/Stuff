[Wind Combatant: Combo Master Lv.1]
At Sato's current level he can't generate wind to attack enemies offensively however he can use it to push certain human limits in the style of combo extensions by modulating wind around his body. Here's an example, Normally in you jump and kick someone, gravity dictates you fall to the ground immediately. However with his wind style of combat he's able to quickly turn his body in the air decreasing his descent to attack a few more times before completely descending towards the ground. There's also the ability for Sato to rush his target if he knocks them a certain distance away from himself in a quick pursuit. Here are the chains listed for his basic actions at this current level.

Air Combo Chain* - Allows Sato to preform three melee actions in the air before descending to the ground.

Pursuit - Currently only works on the ground: Allows Sato to quickly dash after a target after knocking them away with a hard hit. Only allows one melee action for Sato afterwards.
