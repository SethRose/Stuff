Twin Personality
Timothy is able to tap into energy within his body due to an incident in his early childhood. As a result of the incident, his mind created a separate personality in order to cope with the world, Somebody and Nobody. Somebody and Nobody each have their respective skills that can be used at will by themselves. However, it is mainly recommended that they only use their abilities that apply to each personality as using them when they're the other personality attracts consequences.

Nobody
Mask

He is also able to copy the appearance of a deceased being by touching their body only. As for the bodily fluid, when he comes in contact with it, he will be able to copy everything physical from the person. Though Tim will only be able to copy what they currently look like, his transformation won't change if the target's physical appearance changes, forcing him to have to come in contact with their bodily fluid again. This technique is mainly for Nobody and can cause bad side effects if used with Somebody which include vomiting, dry heaving, and wheezing.
