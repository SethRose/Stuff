Flying Warship
Lissandra is able to call upon parts or all of a flying warship to aid her in combat. The warship has the following weapons in its arsenal.
1x Twin Side cannons [2 post CD]
2x Anti-air turrets [1 Post CD]
3x Missile Chutes [3 Post CD]
1x Main Cannon [4 Post CD]
Using one of the weapons attracts a universal cool down until she is able to use another of the weapons again, the main ammunition for the weapons is pure energy.

The warship is also able to be summoned entirely but at the cost of losing functionality of all weapons except the main cannon.

Night Shade Armor


Lissandra is able to equip this armor for a limited time which grants her the following:
Nigh-Superhuman Strength
Enhanced Quickness
Enhanced Durability
Enhanced Speed
Increases Effectiveness of weapons 3x

Stays on for five turns before turning off and holds a cool down of five turns.
