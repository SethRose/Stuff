Abilities & Powers
[Heat Manipulation]
Aurora is able to manipulate the heat levels of objects, including her own body and those of others. This can include raising the temperature to cause fires, and dropping the temperature to around 0. 'course, raising and lowering it to extreme levels takes a lot out of poor old Aurora. Though, if she 'ignites' her body, she doesn't take burn damage. Nor does her body receive any burns, interestingly. She can also drain heat from fires and heat up ice using this 'power'.

[Light Burst]
Her other 'power' involves creating bright flashes of light and sending them forward to create a 'flash bang'. They deal no damage whatsoever, and dissipate after a single post, making them only good for distractions.
